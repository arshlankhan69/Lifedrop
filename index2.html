About LifeDrop

Trusted community-driven donation platform

Why donate?

Each unit of donated blood can save multiple lives. Donating is quick, safe, and deeply impactful.

Safety

We partner with licensed blood banks following standard protocols and safety checks.

Community

Events across the city — meet neighbors and save lives together.

Register to Donate

Sign up, pick a center, and choose a time.

Donation checklist

  • Bring valid ID
  • Eat a healthy meal beforehand
  • Drink plenty of water
  • Wait 12 months if you had major surgery

Eligibility

Generally 18–65 years, 50+ kg, and fit to donate. Check local rules for specifics.

Available Donors

Request Blood

Submit a request and we’ll match you with available donors. Use emergency alert for immediate help.

Matching donors

Use only in life-threatening cases — will notify matched donors immediately.

Receiver Info

We instantly check registered donors against your required blood group using compatibility rules. Emergency alerts simulate contacting donors and create notifications.

  • Only donors you and admins add are matched
  • Your data is saved locally (localStorage)
  • Admin may contact you for verification
